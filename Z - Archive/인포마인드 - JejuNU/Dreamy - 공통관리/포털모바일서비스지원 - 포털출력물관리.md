## 포털/모바일서비스지원 - 포털출력물관리

<br>

```sql
SELECT
	SN
	, TITLE
	, MRD_PATH
	, ID_COLUMN_NM
	, ID_FORMAT
	, MENU_NAV
	, PARAM_PRINT_YN
	, RMRK
	, TO_CHAR(LFSTDT, 'YYYYMMDD') LFSTDT
FROM
	POT_REPORT_LIST
ORDER BY
	MENU_NAV

```
